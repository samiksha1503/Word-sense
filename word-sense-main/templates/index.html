Detect Spam and Abusive Words

What is Spam?

Spam refers to unsolicited and irrelevant messages, often sent in bulk to a large number of recipients. These messages are usually promotional in nature and aim to promote products or services. Spam messages can be annoying and potentially harmful.

What are Abusive Words?

Abusive words, also known as hate speech, are offensive and hurtful language that targets individuals or groups based on their race, ethnicity, gender, religion, or other characteristics. Hate speech can cause emotional distress and contribute to a toxic online environment.
